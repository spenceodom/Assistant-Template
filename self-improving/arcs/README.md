# Arcs

Ongoing life narratives that span multiple sessions. Each arc tracks a major thread in the user's life — work projects, family dynamics, health goals, etc.

## What goes here

Arc files capture:
- **Summary**: What is this arc about?
- **Why it matters**: Stakes, motivations, emotional weight
- **Current status**: Where things stand right now
- **Open threads**: Active questions or next steps
- **Key documents**: Pointers to related files in `knowledge/` or elsewhere

## What does NOT go here

- Operational details (meeting notes, tech specs) — those belong in `domains/` or `knowledge/`
- Resolved history — arcs are forward-looking
- Ephemeral tasks — use journal or tasks for session-level work

## Example arcs

- `career.md` — job transitions, salary negotiations, long-term trajectory
- `family.md` — relationships, milestones, coordination
- `health.md` — fitness goals, medical context, habit tracking
- `project-name.md` — major multi-month initiatives

Create an arc when a topic has enough narrative weight to span multiple sessions and benefits from tracking momentum over time.
